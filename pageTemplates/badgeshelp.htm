meTeen Badges Help

meTeen badges are awards. They are given to members for reaching and maintaining certain requirements. This page outlines common badges, and displays the requirements for acquring them.

Photo Album Badge - Add at least 1 picture to your photo album

Photo Album Enthusiast Badge - Add 10 or more pictures to your photo album

I Heart meTeen Badge - Log into the site once per day for 3 consecutive days

Congeniality Badge - Have atleast 10 friends on meTeen

Popularity Badge - Have atleast 200 profile views

Fame Badge - Have atleast 1000 profile views

AIM Badge - Add your meTeen profile link to your AIM profile (atleast 5 visits to your meTeen profile from your AIM profile)

Social Badge - Post atleast 30 forum posts/threads

Totally Social Badge - Post atleast 100 forum posts/threads

Weekly Update Badge - Add atleast 1 new Blog entry per 7 days

Daily Update Badge - Add atleast 1 new Blog entry per 1 day.
